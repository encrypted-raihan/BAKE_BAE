Our Story

More Than a Bakery —
A Sweet Memory Maker

What started as a passion for baking in the heart of Kottarakkara has grown into a beloved destination for every celebration. At Bake Bae Bakers, we believe every cake tells a story — your story.

From custom birthday cakes crafted to your vision, to freshly baked snacks that feel like home — every item that leaves our kitchen carries the warmth of genuine care and quality ingredients.

Located at Pulamon Junction, we're proud to be Kottarakkara's go-to bakery for life's sweetest moments.
